# High-Risk AI Compliance Kit

Turnkey audit evidence generation for regulated employment AI systems.

## Capabilities

- Counterfactual benchmark datasets
- Bias sensitivity metrics (DIR, subgroup error rates)
- Robustness metrics (counterfactual consistency, worst-group accuracy)
- Traceability artifacts mapped to EU AI Act clauses
- Zipped “Technical File Attachment” evidence bundle
